Training

This chapter provides brief inspiration on how to practice efficiently rather than serving as a comprehensive training guide. The number of possible exercises is virtually endless; however, some general training principles apply, and there are several common exercises used by badminton players. Content is currently being added and will include physical training as well as practice methods for the techniques explained in Footwork.

Simply knowing how to move on court is not enough — it has to be practiced. The section on technical training explains how to train these skills systematically. This includes ways to practice general footwork patterns, movement to and from specific corners, and common movement combinations between corners.

Physical training will be divided into strength training and agility training.
