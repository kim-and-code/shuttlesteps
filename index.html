ShuttleSteps – The Badminton Footwork Lab

Master Singles Movement Step by Step

Hello fellow badminton enthusiasts, and welcome to ShuttleSteps!

How do you move to the forehand rear corner? What sequence of steps should you use, and what are the alternatives? What if you have less time, or need additional steps? How should you move against an attacking clear? How do you neutralize the situation, and how should you recover afterward?

More generally, how do you actually cover the entire court in any given situation?

Have you ever asked yourself questions like these?

I have—all the time.

Badminton has many components, and the technical side in particular can feel overwhelming. But that also means there is a lot to improve, and plenty of room to grow.

This website is dedicated to anyone interested in badminton, especially the technical aspects of singles footwork. I also cover related topics such as strategy, and will continue adding new content.

Over the years, I have benefited greatly from the advice and feedback of coaches and fellow players, from countless practice sessions and tournaments, and from watching others play. I am grateful for all of these insights, and this website is my way of sharing them.

Keep learning, keep practicing, and enjoy the badminton life!
